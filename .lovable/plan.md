

# 🐾 Every Paw Has a Tail to Tell — Website Plan

## Overview
A warm, inviting animal rescue and adoption website starting with **4 core pages** — Home, About Us, Adopt, and Contact — plus a simple **Donate page with a UPI QR code**. Content will be static/hardcoded for a quick launch, with the ability to add a database later.

---

## Pages to Build

### 1. 🏠 Home Page
- **Hero banner** with an emotional tagline ("Every Paw Has a Tail to Tell") and high-impact animal imagery
- **3 call-to-action buttons**: Adopt, Donate, Volunteer
- **Featured animals** section showing 3–4 animals available for adoption (cards with photo, name, breed, age)
- **Impact stats** bar (e.g., "200+ animals rescued", "150+ adoptions", "50+ volunteers")
- **Testimonial snippets** from adopters
- **Quick links** to success stories and news
- **Footer** with contact info, social media links, and navigation

### 2. 📖 About Us Page
- Organization's **story and history**
- **Mission, vision, and values** section
- **Team profiles** with photos and roles
- **Impact numbers** displayed visually (rescued, adopted, volunteers)

### 3. 🐕 Adopt Page
- **Searchable gallery** of animals available for adoption
- **Filters** by species (dog, cat, etc.), age, gender, and size
- **Animal profile cards** with photo, name, breed, age, temperament, and health status
- **Detailed view** (modal or expanded card) with full info and adoption requirements
- **Adoption process steps** explained clearly
- **Adoption inquiry form** (name, email, phone, message — submitted info shown as a toast/confirmation, no backend processing)

### 4. 💰 Donate Page
- Information on **how donations are used** (medical care, food, shelter, etc.)
- **UPI QR code** prominently displayed for easy scanning
- Breakdown of donation impact (e.g., "₹500 feeds a dog for a week")
- **Donor recognition section** (optional — major supporters)

### 5. 📞 Contact Page
- **Contact form** (name, email, subject, message)
- Organization's **address, phone, and email**
- **Embedded map** showing location
- **Emergency rescue contact** information highlighted
- **Social media links** (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X)

---

## Design & Style
- **Color palette**: Warm tones — soft orange, earthy green, cream/off-white backgrounds, with paw-print accents
- **Typography**: Friendly, rounded fonts for headings; clean sans-serif for body text
- **Imagery**: Large, emotive animal photos throughout
- **UI Elements**: Rounded corners, soft shadows, playful icons (paw prints, hearts)
- **Fully responsive**: Mobile-first design that works beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktops

---

## Navigation
- **Sticky top navbar** with logo and links: Home, About, Adopt, Donate, Contact
- **Mobile hamburger menu** for smaller screens
- **Footer** with sitemap links, social icons, and contact info
